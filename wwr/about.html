History

Standing Wave is the premier whitewater rafting company in the area. We offer fun, relaxation, and a thrilling trip on rivers like you've never seen before. With three different rivers with varying levels of adventure to choose from, we can give anyone of a mature age an experience that will last a lifetime. The joy of life comes when we encounter new experiences, especially those that are filled with fun and excitement. Sleep under a million stars and wake up to the smell of crisp fresh mountain air. Spend the day in the sun laughing with friends and family as you paddle down sensational rapids and experience all the delights and thrills of whitewater rafting.
